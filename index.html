about me info

As a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems student, I am deeply focused on web development, project management, data analysis, and system structures. I focus on designing efficient, user-centered digital solutions that bridge business requirements with technology-driven outcomes.

Through academic projects and hands-on experience as a clinic programmer, I have developed responsive web applications, managed project workflows, analyzed operational data, and designed structured information systems that support organizational processes. These projects have strengthened my ability to develop scalable systems, manage project deliverables, analyze business data, and implement effective system structures that enhance operational efficiency.
